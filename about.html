About Me

I am an industrial designer who works with a variety of mediums, including wood, textiles, ceramics, 3D design, and more. My passion for design began in high school, where I discovered my drive to create products that make life more enjoyable and effortless for people. I love blending conventional elements with a touch of uniqueness to bring fresh perspectives to my work. I believe my versatile skills and approach will enable me to create designs that are both personal and meaningful, while remaining adaptable for collaboration with other designers and users. Check out my portfolio to explore my ideas and see how I turn them into feasible designs.
